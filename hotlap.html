Hot Lap Leaderboards

Hot Lap Rating (name can be changed)

The Hot Lap Rating sums up your best laps from each track to give you your overall performance compared to other drivers.

If no lap is recorded for a given track, a time of 5 minutes (5:00) is added to your Hot Lap Rating for this specific course.

Driver with lowest total time is considered the fastest - so it's worth to complete laps on every track to get the best standing.
